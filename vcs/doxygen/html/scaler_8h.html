◆ SCALER_FUNC_PARAMS

#define SCALER_FUNC_PARAMS   u8 *const pixelData, const resolution_s &sourceRes, const resolution_s &targetRes

The parameters of scaling functions.

pixelData are the pixels of the image to be scaled.

sourceRes is the original resolution of the pixel data.

targetRes is the resolution to which the pixel data are to be scaled. This can be either larger or smaller than sourceRes.
